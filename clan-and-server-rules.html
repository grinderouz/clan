CLAN RULES

Grinderouz Official Code of Conduct

:xp:

 Rules Agreement

1

Activity is Key

We value active players. While we understand life happens, extended periods of inactivity without notice may result in a demotion or being kicked from the clan to make room for active members.

2

Removal Policy

The leadership team reserves the right to kick any member at any time. We don't do this randomly; a reason will be provided (e.g., missed war attacks, toxic behavior, or consistent low donations).

3

Rule Evolution

As the clan grows, our needs might change. We reserve the right to update or modify these rules at any time. We will do our best to post an announcement in the clan chat whenever a significant change is made.

:clan:

 Community & Behavior

1

INACTIVITY

Players inactive for 7 consecutive days without notifying leadership will be kicked.

2

Zero Tolerance Policy

Harassment, racism, or excessive toxicity towards clan mates will result in an immediate kick. We are a family.

3

English Only

To ensure effective communication during wars, please use English in the main clan chat.

4

No Rushed Bases

When and during clan wars. Rushed bases is a easy target to stars and makes you less powerful.

:warleag:

 War Protocol

1

Use All Attacks

If you are opted "In" for war, you MUST use both attacks. Missing attacks without prior notice will lead to a demotion or kick.

2

Follow the Plan

Check Clan Mail or Discord for specific targets. Do not attack for loot unless the war is mathematically won.

3

No Heroes, No War

Standard wars require Heroes to be up.

:gold:

 Clan Games & Raids

1

The Bare Minimum

Minimum 1000 points required. We always aim for the top tier.

2

Capital Raids

Use all 5-6 attacks every weekend. Finish the district you start.

 :troopdonations:

Donations & Requests

1

Read Before You Donate

Always donate exactly what is requested.

2

Upgrading

Strictly MAX level troops only for War CCs and War attacks.

3

Ratios

No strict ratios, but heavy leeching without giving back will result in a warning.

:discord:

 Discord Rules

Click Here!
1

Verification

Unverified users cannot use bots or see core channels. Verify by being in the clan or requesting member role.

2

Stay on Topic

Use specific channels (#base-designs, #bot-commands) for their intended purpose.

3

Keep it Clean

No NSFW content, intense politics, or religious debates. & No hate speech or toxicity. Banter is fine; harassment is an instant ban.

4

LOCKED-IN SERVER PROTECTION

Members in our Discord who are a verified member will be protected from sudden kicks and will instead recieve warnings through the ProBot System.

 Rank & Promotions

Apply For a Elder Now!
1

Don't Ask for Ranks

Elder and Co-Leader positions are earned through loyalty, activity, and high donation counts. Asking for rank decreases your chances.

2

Elder Requirements

1000+ Donations total and 100% participation in consecutive wars. Been in Clan over 1 Month or Longer.
